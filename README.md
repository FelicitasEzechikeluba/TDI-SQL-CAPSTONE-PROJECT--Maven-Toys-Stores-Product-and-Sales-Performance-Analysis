# Maven Toys Product and Sales Performance Analysis

SQL analysis of product and sales data from Maven Toys, a fictitious toy retail chain with 4 stores across 29 cities in Mexico.

## About

This project analyzes sales, inventory, and store performance to identify top-selling products, sales trends, and factors influencing profitability. The goal is to provide insights for better inventory planning, pricing strategies, and promotional decisions.

## Dataset

- **Source**: Maven Analytics
- **Records**: 829,263 transactions
- **Tables**: Products, Stores, Sales, Inventory
- **Period**: 2022-2023

## Key Results

- **Total Revenue**: $14.44M
- **Total Profit**: $4.01M
- **Best Category**: Art & Crafts (325,574 units sold)
- **Top Product**: Colorbuds
- **Peak Season**: Q2 (April-June) and December
- **Best Store**: Ciudad de Mexico 2
